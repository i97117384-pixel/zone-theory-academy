Theory

Core concepts to master

The building blocks of professional supply & demand and SMC analysis.

📦

Supply & Demand Zones

Price returns to areas where institutions placed unfilled orders. These zones mark the real support and resistance — not the lines retail traders draw.

Origin CandleZone FreshnessDistal / Proximal
💧

Liquidity Pools

Equal highs, equal lows, stop clusters above swing points — price seeks these areas to fill institutional orders before reversing.

EQH / EQLBSL / SSLStop Hunt
📐

Market Structure

BOS confirms continuation. CHoCH signals a potential reversal. Reading structure gives you context before entering any trade.

BOSCHoCHMSS
⚖️

Premium & Discount

The 50% equilibrium line splits every range. Buying in discount and selling in premium is the institutional way.

Fibonacci 50%EquilibriumOTE
🔲

Order Blocks & FVG

The last candle before an impulse move hides institutional footprints. Fair Value Gaps show where price moved too fast and needs to return.
